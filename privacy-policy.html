Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 21, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Claro is designed to keep your personal and financial data under your control — we don't sell it, we don't advertise with it, and we never will.

1 Who We Are

Claro is developed and operated by Mert Türk as an independent application. For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at mertturk0013@gmail.com.

2 Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address used to create and manage your account.
  • Financial data: Income and expense transactions, categories, monthly budget limits, savings goals, and net worth entries you manually enter.
  • Habit data: Habits you create, daily completion records, streaks, streak freezes used, and reminder times you configure.
  • App preferences: Theme selection, currency setting, and notification preferences.
  • Device & diagnostic data: Crash reports and anonymous performance diagnostics via Sentry.
  • Subscription status: Whether your account is Free or Premium. We do not store or access your payment card details.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your data across devices (cloud sync).
  • To send daily habit reminders at the times you explicitly set.
  • To process and manage your subscription status via RevenueCat.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability.
  • To restore your data when you reinstall or switch devices.

We do not use your financial or habit data for advertising, profiling, or sale to any third party.

4 Push Notifications

If you configure a daily reminder for a habit, Claro will send a push notification at the time you selected. You can disable notifications at any time from your device's system settings, or by turning off reminders inside the app.

5 Third-Party Services

Claro relies on the following third-party providers, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Supabase — authentication & database RevenueCat — subscription management Sentry — crash & error reporting Apple — app distribution & payments

None of these providers receive your raw financial or habit data for their own use.

6 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure and protected with industry-standard encryption — TLS in transit and AES-256 at rest. Data persisted locally on your device uses the platform's secure storage mechanisms.

7 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account), all associated data is permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days.

8 Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data directly in the app, or by contacting us.
  • Notification opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time via system or app settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email mertturk0013@gmail.com.

9 Children's Privacy

Claro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
