Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 17, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Claro is designed to keep your personal and financial data under your control. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service and never sell your data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

Claro is developed and operated by Mert Türk ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at mertturk0013@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Your email address when you register or sign in.
  • Financial data: Transactions, income, expense categories, savings goals, and net worth entries that you manually input into the app.
  • Habit data: Habits you create, completion logs, streaks, and reminders you configure.
  • Device & usage data: Crash reports and anonymous diagnostic information to help us fix bugs and improve stability (via Sentry).
  • Purchase information: Subscription status and transaction receipts, processed by RevenueCat and Apple. We do not store your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and sync your data across devices.
  • To provide core app features: habit tracking, expense tracking, savings goals, and net worth.
  • To process your subscription and verify premium access.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app performance.
  • To send you optional habit reminder notifications (only if you enable them).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Supabase – Authentication and cloud database (data stored in secure EU/US servers). Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat – In-app subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry – Crash reporting and error monitoring (no personally identifiable data is sent). Privacy Policy
  • Apple – App distribution, payment processing, and push notifications. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase's cloud infrastructure using industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest). We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app, all your personal data (account, habits, transactions, goals) is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for notifications at any time via your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at mertturk0013@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Claro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
